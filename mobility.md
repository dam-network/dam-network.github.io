If you are an early-career researcher in Spain and working on a topics related to the ones of this Network, you may be eligible to receive support for a research stay at one of the [Network’s nodes](https://dam-network.github.io/groups/). These stays provide an excellent opportunity to strengthen collaborations, exchange knowledge and expertise, and expand your professional network within the community. To learn more about the available support and the application process, please contact the network coordinator Guillem Perarnau at guillem.perarnau@upc.edu.
