# [BIOCOM: Grup de Biologia Computacional i Bioinformàtica (UIB)](https://www.uib.cat/recerca/estructures/estructura/BIOCOM/)

# Universitat de les Illes Balears

![image](/directory-groups/biocom.jpeg)

## Research Interests

Combinatorics and graph theory applied to biology; algorithms and complexity in computational biology; phylogenetic networks; metrics and invariants for trees and networks; network reconstruction and generation; analysis and comparison of biological networks (metabolic and protein–protein interaction networks); classification and analysis of metagenomic and metaviromic data; statistics and machine learning for omics data.
