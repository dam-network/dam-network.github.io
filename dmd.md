The conference series *Discrete Mathematics Days (DMD)* is  the largest national event in the field and serves as an international meeting point for researchers. The series is organized by the DAM network. Known as DMD since 2016, this conference inherits the tradition of the Jornadas de Matemática Discreta y Algorítmica (JMDA), the Spanish biennial meeting on Discrete Mathematics started in 1998.
The main focus of this international conference is on current topics in Discrete Mathematics, including (but not limited to):

- Coding Theory and Cryptography.
- Combinatorial Number Theory.
- Combinatorics.
- Discrete and Computational Geometry.
- Discrete Optimization.
- Graph Theory.
- Theoretical Computer Science.
